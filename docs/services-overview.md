# Page Blueprint: Services Overview

## Page Goal
Present UGC's full capability at a glance. Route visitors to the specific service that matches their need. Signal "one partner for everything."

## Target Audience Intent
"What do they offer?" · "Do they cover my specific need?" · "Can I use one company for all my logistics?"

## Content Sections

**1. Hero**
Light bg. Heading: "One Partner for All Your Supply Chain Needs" (or localized editorial variant). Brief supporting text: one sentence about integrated logistics.

**2. Service Grid**
Full editorial grid — all 6 services. Larger treatment than home page grid since this is the dedicated services page. Each card: number, name, tag (e.g., "Land · Sea · Air"), 2-sentence description, key stat or differentiator, "Learn More →" link. Cards use flat variant on light bg with hover lift.

**3. Cross-Service Value Statement**
Brief section: "Why use one logistics partner?" Explain the integration benefit — single point of contact, coordinated schedules, unified documentation, consistent quality standards.

**4. CTA Band**
"Not sure which service fits your needs?" + "Talk to our team" CTA → quote form or WhatsApp.

## CTA Logic
Service cards → service detail pages. CTA band → quote form. Header persistent.

## Trust Logic
Service breadth signals comprehensive capability. Cross-service statement builds the partnership narrative.

## Motion
Staggered card reveal. CTA fade-in.

## SEO Intent
Keywords: "jasa logistik indonesia," "one stop logistics solution." BreadcrumbList schema.

## Analytics Events
`page_view`, `scroll_depth`, `service_card_click` (per service), `cta_click_quote_inline`

## Recirculation
All 6 service detail pages, About, Quote.
