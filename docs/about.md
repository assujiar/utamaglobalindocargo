# Page Blueprint: About

## Page Goal
Build trust through narrative. Make UGC feel real, experienced, accountable. Visitor transitions from "who are they?" to "I trust them."

## Target Audience Intent
"Are they credible?" · "How long have they been around?" · "What makes them different?"

## Content Sections

**1. Hero**
Light bg. Display serif headline: philosophy statement ("Integrity in Every Mile" or similar). Breadcrumb: Home > About.

**2. Origin Story**
200-300 word narrative. Not a timeline — a story: why UGC started, how it evolved, what stayed constant. Warm, honest, specific. Paired with logo or typographic treatment.

**3. Operating Philosophy**
"We Care What We Deliver" expanded into 3-4 operational principles. Each: heading + 2-3 sentence explanation + specific example or metric.

**4. Network & Credentials**
WCA, IATA, 150+ countries, 34 provinces. Presented with context ("our WCA membership means a vetted agent at every major port, not a directory listing").

**5. Client Stories Section**
2-3 Client Story cards sourced from Supabase (tag-matched to diverse industries). Each card: industry tag, challenge summary, result highlight, "Read more" expand or link. No company names or logos. This section replaces the traditional client logo strip. Stories demonstrate capability across industries without revealing client identities.

**6. CTA Section**
"Explore Our Services" secondary + "Request a Quote" primary.

## CTA Logic
CTA section → Services (routing) + Quote (conversion). Header persistent.

## Trust Logic
Origin story (narrative) → Philosophy (operational) → Credentials (institutional) → Client Stories (evidence-based proof)

## Motion
Hero: fade-in. Story section: scroll-reveal paragraphs. Credentials: staggered badge reveal. Client Stories: staggered card reveal. Minimal — this is a reading page.

## Desktop Logic
Story: 7/5 asymmetric (text left, visual right). Client Stories: 3-column card row.

## Mobile Logic
Single column throughout. Client Stories stacked vertically.

## SEO Intent
Keywords: "about ugc logistics," "profil perusahaan ekspedisi." H1 = philosophy headline.

## Analytics Events
`page_view`, `scroll_depth`, `cta_click_quote_inline`, `recirculation_click`

## Recirculation
Services overview, featured insight, Quote.
